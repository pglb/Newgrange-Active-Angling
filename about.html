About Me

My name is Brendan O''Reilly and I've been fishing about 30 years. I started fishing on the River Nanny in Duleek for trout. I also fished the Boyne for Bream, Perch and Eels. As the years went by, I became a member of ''Rosin and Slane'' for Salmon and Seatrout, the 'Dee and Glyde'' for Seatrout and Wild Browns and ''Duleek Angling Club'' where I fish The National Pike Competitions for the Duleek club.

I am a TTT Fetac 6 holder and an ACI - Angling Council of Ireland - trainee coach.
